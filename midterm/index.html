My favorite seasons from greatest to least

Winter
I love the winter time so much. Cold air feels so nice and snow is cool, but not for driving in. Also my winter wardrobe is so much cuter than my spring or summer since layering is really cute. The cold is just superior to the other temperatures during the year and no annoying leaved to deal with or pollen.
Fall
Fall is cool and all but it's not as cool as winter. Leaves annoy me and I don't like raking them, even if i get paid for it. And the leaves all fall at once so one day your yard could look great then the next day its like someone took every leaf from the world and put it in front of your house. Also it rains all the time and rain is nice but not when its freezing cold.
Summer
Ok, summer is nice but I hate the heat. A little bit of heat is nice but the oppressive heat in like July and August is disgusting and I dont't like it. I already get sweaty from working out but when I run in the summertime it looks like I jumped in a lake which is nasty. Also my skin is super sensitive to the sun which isn't fun. The beach is cool too but I dont like sand touching me so that's a minor problem. Like sand is ok but I hate how it sticks to your body after you go in the ocean or bay.
Spring
Whoever like the springtime is either a farmer or straight crazy. Literally its all rainy, humid, and moist. There is so much pollen in the air and my allergies go from zero to four billion during the spring. Spring is like the annoying middle child of the seasons. It's the season no one really likes but has to put up with until summer starts. I really hate spring with every cell in my body..
